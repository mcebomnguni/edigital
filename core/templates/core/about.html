Who We Are

Built for South Africans,
by the Department of Home Affairs

eDigital provides citizens with a single, secure portal to view, manage, and verify their government-issued identity documents — from Smart IDs to birth certificates.

Our Mission

To provide all South African citizens with secure, accessible digital identity solutions that simplify document management, improve government service delivery, and protect personal data.

Our Vision

A fully digital South Africa where every citizen has instant, verified access to their identity credentials — enabling seamless interaction with both public and private sector services.

Security First

Enterprise-Grade Protection

Fernet Encryption

All document data is encrypted at rest using symmetric Fernet encryption with a government-managed key.

Two-Factor Auth

Every login is secured with a TOTP one-time password sent to your registered email address.

Document Integrity

HMAC-SHA256 signatures ensure every document record is tamper-evident and cryptographically verified.

Session Security

Sessions expire after 30 minutes of inactivity. HttpOnly, SameSite cookies prevent hijacking.

Biometric Verification

Production deployments integrate with Azure Face API for facial recognition at login.

Audit Logging

Every document access, login event, and admin action is recorded in a tamper-proof activity log.
